Causes of Gaming Addiction

Escapism: Seeking refuge from stress, anxiety, depression, or real-life challenges.

Social Isolation: Finding connection and belonging in the virtual world.

Low Self-Esteem: Seeking validation and accomplishment within the game.

Underlying Mental Health: Conditions like anxiety and depression can increase vulnerability.

Effects of Gaming Addiction

Mental Health: Increased anxiety, depression, and sleep issues.

Social: Social isolation, strained relationships, difficulty interacting.

Academic/Professional: Poor performance, job loss.

Physical: Sedentary lifestyle, poor diet, sleep deprivation.

Financial: Excessive spending, financial instability.
